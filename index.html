Who I Am

A sanctuary for skin that deserves intentional care

I created LB Skin Sanctuary from a deep belief that skincare should be more than a routine — it should be a ritual. I'm a holistic esthetician rooted in Long Beach, CA, and I'm dedicated to treating your skin through a whole-body lens.

Every client I work with receives my full, undivided attention. I take time to understand your lifestyle, stress levels, and skin history — because I know that real, lasting results begin beneath the surface. When you're in my chair, you'll never feel rushed or sold to. Just heard, cared for, and seen.

My Mission

My passion for clean skincare deepened when I faced cancer and experienced firsthand how profoundly what we put on our bodies matters. Healing taught me the power of intentional touch, the importance of truly knowing what goes on your skin, and how deeply connected our outer health is to everything within. LB Skin Sanctuary is the culmination of that journey — a space where I share what I've learned, care for each client with honesty and intention, and help others feel empowered in their own skin health story.
